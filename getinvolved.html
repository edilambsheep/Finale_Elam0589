Donations − Membership − Volunteer

Donation

As a non for profit organisation, we receive no government funding. All our revenue comes from memberships and plant sales.

If you would like to make a one-off donation, we'd greatly appreciate it!

Membership

We currently have over 1,400 members across NSW, Australia and the world! Are you thinking about becoming a member? Fill out the form, and you will receive the following benefits and many more:

  • Receive discounts at participating plant nurseries and organisations, because, who would want to have discounts on plants?
  • Receive exclusive monthly e-newsletters about native plants.
  • Receive quarterly issues of Australian native plant journals.
  • Immersing yourself in Australian Plants Society NSW activities.

Volunteer

Our organisation is driven not only by members but also amazing volunteers. Are you thinking of contributing to Australian native plants? This is an excellent opportunity to offer your time to assist at exciting events that involve plant propagation, undertaking conservation activities and writing submissions!
