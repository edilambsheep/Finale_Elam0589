About Us

Whether you're living in the heart of Sydney, or rural New South Wales, there will always be Australian native plants within reach. Do you ever notice those fuzzy yellow flowers that bloom on trees during spring? That's most likely, an Acacia pycnantha, or more commonly known as the golden wattle, which if you guessed correctly, is an Australian native plant.

Home to some of the resilient floras in the world, adapting to harsh climates we're all commonly familiar with, we, as the Australian Plant Society NSW, established back in 1957, aim to promote the beauty of Australian flora.

Made up your mind already? Join us!

Our vision is to be the first point of call for information and advice about Australian native plants their habitats.

Our mission is to create a network of members, ranging from all ages, with a passion for learning about, growing, and conserving Australian native plants and their habitats.

Our values comprise of integrity, credibility, respect and enthusiasm.
